The video studio is a powerful tool that tracks channel analytics, providing valuable insights into the performance of videos and channels on the platform. This page includes various metrics, such as views, engagement rate, and timezones. It allows content creators to analyse their channel's performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to optimise their content.

The video analytics page offers an intuitive user interface, making it easy for content creators to navigate through the data and access the information they need. The page also provides data visualisation tools enabling content creators to quickly identify trends and patterns in their data. Moreover, the video analytics page includes real-time data, allowing content creators to monitor their channel's performance in real-time. Overall, the video analytics page for a video streaming platform is a valuable tool that provides insights into the performance of channels and videos. By analysing this data, content creators can make informed decisions to optimise their content, increase engagement, and grow their audience.

List of features include

  1. Channel analytics -> Track total comments, views, likes, dislikes, Subscribers. Ability to specify and track the timeline of data in a comparative manner. Track timezone chart.
  2. Manage Videos -> Manage, edit, delete and track individual video analytics with timezones and viewership counts.
  3. Comment Tracker -> Isolate and track just the comments and comment analytics with the ability to specify time.
  4. Earnings -> Track earnings based on the day, month and total. This page has a timezone chart and tables of earnings which will display entries of User ID, payer name, commission, time, video, amount, Net earnings.
  5. Manage Movies - > Manage, edit, delete movies.
