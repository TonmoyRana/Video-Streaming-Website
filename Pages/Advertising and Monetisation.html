The software allows you to have a pay to view feature for uploaded content. Content uploading users can decide to set a price on any video for users to pay to view.

You can set up all types of global currencies via Paypal which is integrated onto the system. Other payment modes are Local Bank Transfers, Credit and Debit cards.

Some of the specific payment modes include the following

  1. 2Checkout (Credit Cards) Payment Method
  2. Stripe (Credit Cards) Payment Method
  3. PayStack (Credit Cards) Payment Method
  4. RazorPay (Credit Cards) Payment Method
  5. CashFree (Credit Cards) Payment Method
  6. Iyzipay Payment Method
  7. PaySera Payment Method
  8. PayU Payment Method
